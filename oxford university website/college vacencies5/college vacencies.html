BLACK FRIARS BRASENOSE COLLEGE

Oxford University’s knowledge exchange community is united by a common purpose: to accelerate the impact of research on the world. We bring: passionate people research excellenc an innovative culture We seek: connections genuine partnerships inspiring possibilities We achieve: mutual reward impact on the real world How it works You can engage with us in a variety of ways, from accessing an individual’s expertise to partnering with us long-term.
